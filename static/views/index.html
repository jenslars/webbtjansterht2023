About Youtube to Spotify

Youtube to Spotify is a service that helps you create Spotify playlists by providing a URL to a Youtube playlist or video with songs.

How to use:

To identify a single song, enter the URL of a song you'd like to identify. Example

To identify several songs in a video, enter the URL of the video.

To convert a Youtube playlist, make sure the URL is in fact the URL to the playlist itself and not the URL to any of the songs in the playlist.
